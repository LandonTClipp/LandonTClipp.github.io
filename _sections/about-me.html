I'm a native resident of Champaign, Illinois and have always had a deep passion and respect for computers. I graduated from the University of Illinois in 2018 with a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering, and also received an Associate's in Engineering Sciences from Parkland College. Besides computers, I also have a deep passion for aviation. At the age of 13, I began paying for flight lessons and eventually ended up buying my own Powered Parachute at the age of 15.

Additionally, I'm a hobbiest in all things alcohol: spirits, wines, cocktails, beer, and cordials. At no point would I claim to be an expert, but I sure can make a fancy Manhattan!
