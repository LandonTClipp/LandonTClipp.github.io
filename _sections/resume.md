I currently work as a high performance computing system administrator for Jump Trading, a high frequency trading firm. I graduated from the U of I in 2018 with a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering after working for the National Center for Supercomputing Applications for over two years developing big data software on the Blue Waters Supercomputer for atmospheric science resesarch. My specializations primarily revolve around high performance computing, including parallel programming (CUDA, MPI, OpenMP), system administration, big data management, and software development. 

One of the largest motivations for me to study computers is to advanced the scientific pursuit. I take great pride and satisfaction in work that helps to further scientific research. I enjoy fields such as quantum physics, cosmology, astrophysics, climatology, and biology.
