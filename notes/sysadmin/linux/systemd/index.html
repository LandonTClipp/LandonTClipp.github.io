    Verify a systemd calendar spec¶

    Text Only
    $ systemd-analyze calendar '12:*:*'
Original form: 12:*:*
Normalized form: *-*-* 12:*:*
    Next elapse: Tue 2023-12-12 12:00:00 CST
       (in UTC): Tue 2023-12-12 18:00:00 UTC
       From now: 13h left
